🧵 Why Tailoring Matters to Me

Tailoring isn’t just what I do. It’s who I am. There’s something magical about taking a piece of fabric and shaping it into something that fits not just the body, but the personality, the moment, the story. Whether I’m adjusting a hem or crafting a bespoke suit from scratch, I’m not just sewing. I’m listening. I’m translating someone’s style, confidence, and individuality into stitches.

Sohna's Creation Founder

Hi, I’m Saihou and tailoring isn’t just my profession. It’s my passion, my craft, and my way of helping people feel truly seen. Every stitch I make is guided by one simple belief: your clothes should fit your body, your style, and your story. Whether it’s a suit for a milestone moment or a beloved dress that needs new life, I treat every garment like it matters because it does.

Sohna's Creation Founder

I didn’t inherit a fashion empire or study in a design school abroad. I started with curiosity, a needle, and a deep desire to create something meaningful. I remember the first time I altered a shirt for a friend it wasn’t perfect, but it fit better. And more importantly, they smiled. That moment sparked something in me. Since then, I’ve spent years learning, practicing, and perfecting the art of tailoring. From classic menswear and womenswear to modern silhouettes, I’ve worked with fabrics of all kinds and people from all walks of life. And every time, the goal is the same: to make you feel confident in what you wear.
